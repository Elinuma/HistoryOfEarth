Phanerozoic Eon

The current geological eon, Phanerozoic Eon. This eon occurs to the span of 541 million (which is the end of Proterozoic Eon) to the present. Phanerozoic Eon is also considered as the eon of all visible life as this eon occurs all the life we can see as of now. It is divided into three major divisions; Paleozoic Era, Mesozoic Era, and Cenozoic Era.
